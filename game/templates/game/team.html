MEET THE TEAM


Raquel Torres Martínez — Myself
  As the developer and visionary behind Baltito Game, I bring the creative passion, technical skills, and dedication to bring this unique psychological adventure to life. My deep connection to the wilderness and a desire to explore the emotional world of animals fuel the heart of the game.




Balto — The Inspiration
  Balto, my loyal companion and muse, is more than just a dog, he’s the soul of the game. His curiosity, bravery, and love of nature inspire every moment of Baltito Game. By watching him explore the world, I've created an emotional, immersive journey that mirrors his spirit. Together, me and Balto are crafting a narrative that celebrates the connection between humans, animals, and the wild.
